For over 30 years, Thomas Metal Supply has manufactured all of your metal roofing and guttering supplies. Let us help you redesign your home with our metal roofing, guttering, vinyl siding, and windows. It will make your home look 20 years younger.﻿

We custom make all your Metal Roofing & Trim in our own warehouse in Cleveland, TN! No more piecing together your ridge cap, gable rakes, or any other trim needed for your roofing.
